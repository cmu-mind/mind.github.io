CMU Lab for AI and Reinforcement learning

Join Us

PhD researchers: The lab is constantly looking for self-motivated and talented students to work towards pushing the boundaries of reinforcement learning, decision-making, and AI. If you are a prospective PhD or MS student, thank you for your interest! Please apply through various CMU SCS PhD programs (ML, CS, LTI, RI, etc). The lab can recruit students from all of these programs. Please do not email the PI or students before the admissions. If you are already admitted to a CMU PhD program and might want to explore the possibility of working in the lab, please email Prof. Kumar with your resume and a short description of your interests.

MS and Undergraduate researchers: If you are a current MS or undergraduate student in SCS at CMU, and are interested in getting involved in our research, please apply to the lab using this form: XX (this form requires a CMU login). We periodically update this form and will indicate if a particular opening becomes available. We recruit undergraduate researchers at all class levels, though a background in AI and machine learning, as well as excellent grades, are preferred. We typically interview new undergraduate researchers at the beginning of the semester but we might also interview students at other times.

Visitors and Post-Docs: If you are not a current CMU student, chances are that at least in academic year 2024-2025, we will likely not be able to support you for in-person visits. However, we might be able to support some remote collaborations. If you are interested in this option, please send over an email of what you are interested in along with a rough background to Prof. Kumar.
